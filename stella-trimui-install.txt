-------------------------------------------
-------- Stella for Trimui Model S --------
-------------------------------------------
0. Requirements
-------------------------------------------
Upgraded firmware to 0.105
MinUI as launcher
-------------------------------------------
1. Install
-------------------------------------------
Copy the content of the release ZIP on
your Trimui SD card.

Emus/Atari2600.pak contains the emulator
executable and the launch.sh script.

Drop your games (.a26 or .zip) in Roms/Atari2600

You need to add at least one game to make the
Atari2600 emulator shows up in MinUI.

Emulator snapshots are stored in the folder :
Roms/Atari2600/.stella/states
-------------------------------------------
2. Update
-------------------------------------------
Copy the content of the release ZIP on
your Trimui SD card, Depending of your system,
Say yes to "replace all" or "Merge folders"
-------------------------------------------
3. Uninstall
-------------------------------------------
Remove folders :
Emus/Atari2600.pak
Roms/Atari2600
